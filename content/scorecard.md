If your team keeps asking which number is the real one, this scorecard is for you.

It is built for Shopify and ecommerce operators who need to understand whether the problem is revenue-signal trust, attribution confidence, conversion friction, reporting drag, or a combination of them.

You will get:

- an immediate score
- a short diagnosis
- a next step that matches the level of risk
- a follow-up path if the result needs a deeper conversation

This is not a maturity test for vanity metrics. It is a quick way to see whether your revenue signal is strong enough to support confident commercial decisions.
