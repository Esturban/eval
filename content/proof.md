EV Advisory is built for operators who need the signal to be trustworthy enough to act on.

The proof pages below are the clearest examples of that work:

- [Reporting Time Compression]({{< relref "insights/resources/case-study-bsg-lighting-fast-reporting" >}}): reduced recurring reporting time from 30 hours per month to 5 minutes, saving more than $4,000 monthly in operating drag.
- [Lifecycle Revenue Recovery]({{< relref "insights/resources/case-study-harry-rosen-nurturing-clients" >}}): generated more than $100,000 in three months from clients who were already in the ecosystem.

## What these outcomes are meant to show

They are not random wins. Each one reflects the same operating principle:

- improve the quality of the signal
- reduce the friction around the decision
- make the commercial action easier to defend and easier to repeat
