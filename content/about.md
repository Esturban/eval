EV Advisory exists for teams that keep asking a costly question: which number is the real one?

When Shopify, GA4, ad platforms, and finance tell different stories, operators slow down. Budget decisions get harder to defend. Conversion problems stay fuzzy. Reporting becomes a weekly negotiation instead of a reliable operating rhythm.

That is the gap EV Advisory is built to close.

## What EV Advisory does

EV Advisory helps ecommerce and Shopify teams rebuild trust in the signal behind growth decisions. Most engagements begin in one of four places:

- reporting trust across Shopify, analytics, ad platforms, and finance
- attribution noise that makes channel spend difficult to defend
- conversion friction that is visible in outcomes but unclear in cause
- reporting and operations drag that slows weekly decision-making

The work is diagnostic first. The goal is to make the commercial story easier to trust, easier to explain, and easier to act on.

## Who leads the work

EV Advisory is led by **Esteban Valencia**, a data and growth operator who works across strategy, implementation, and operating design.

The practice is intentionally narrow. It is not trying to be a general-purpose marketing agency. The focus is on the layer where ecommerce teams lose confidence:

- the relationship between measurement and decision-making
- the relationship between site friction and commercial performance
- the relationship between reporting quality and operating speed

## How the work is approached

EV Advisory does not start by adding more dashboards. It starts by identifying where trust breaks.

That usually means:

1. clarifying the real decision the team is trying to make
2. tracing where the supporting signal becomes noisy or contradictory
3. identifying the highest-risk gaps in tracking, attribution, reporting, or journey design
4. turning those findings into a tighter reporting and decision system

The output should feel practical. Operators should end up with fewer unresolved arguments, not more documentation.

## Why teams hire EV Advisory

The value is usually not that data suddenly exists. The value is that decisions become easier once the noise is reduced.

Teams tend to reach out when:

- performance reviews are dominated by reconciliation instead of action
- channel results are hard to defend because attribution is unstable
- growth teams suspect conversion friction but cannot isolate where it lives
- reporting is still manual enough to steal time from real operating work
