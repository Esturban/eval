This case study is about a simple but expensive problem: too much operating time was being spent rebuilding the same reporting view every month.

## The problem

The business had recurring reporting needs, but the workflow was still manual enough to consume roughly 30 hours every month.

That meant:

- time was being spent assembling the reporting instead of using it
- recurring reviews depended on fragile exports and handoffs
- the reporting process itself had become an operating drag

## The EV Advisory focus

The work centered on reporting redesign, not dashboard theater.

The goal was to reduce:

- manual assembly time
- repeated reconciliation work
- the number of steps required before a usable performance view existed

## The outcome

The result was a reporting workflow that compressed recurring effort from approximately 30 hours per month to 5 minutes.

That translated into:

- more than $4,000 in monthly operating savings
- faster access to the performance story
- more room for the team to spend time on action instead of assembly

This is the kind of outcome EV Advisory targets when reporting drag is the main commercial bottleneck.
